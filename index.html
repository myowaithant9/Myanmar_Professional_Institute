Myanmar Professional Institute

Mission

The mission of Myanmar Professional Institute is to provide each student a diverse education in a safe, supportive environment that promotes self-discipline, motivation, and excellence in learning.

Vision

Inspired by Myanmar Professional Institute (MPI) will provide the highest quality programs, developing ideas to nourish the body, mind and soul of each student, and help them become professionals in the future.
